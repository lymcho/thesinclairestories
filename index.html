The Sinclaire Stories is a home for original dark fiction — psychological thrillers, domestic noir, and literary suspense — written as immersive novels and produced as cinematic audiobook experiences.

Each story is written, narrated, and produced from scratch — manuscript to final mix. Voice, score, silence, all placed by hand. The kind of fiction that sounds like it's sitting too close.

The Author

L.Z. Sinclaire

Psychological and domestic thrillers about women caught in beautiful traps — of privilege, obsession, and desire.

Her Sinclaire Stories are sharp, character-driven suspense novels where the scariest dangers aren't monsters in the dark, but the elegant systems and relationships that quietly close in around her characters. From Lincoln Park brownstones to Chinatown sublets, Gold Coast high-rises to Seattle tech lofts — each story is written, narrated, and produced from the ground up.
