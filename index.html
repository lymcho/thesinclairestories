About

Stories that stay with you

The Sinclaire Stories is a home for original dark fiction — psychological thrillers, domestic noir, and literary suspense — written as immersive novels and produced as cinematic audiobook experiences.

Every title is crafted from the ground up: from manuscript to audiobook narration, sound design, and atmospheric presentation. No recycled content. No algorithms. Just carefully built fiction meant to unsettle — on the page and in your headphones.
